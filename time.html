there are many other uses of mathematics for time management & scheduling.

Behind the World's Computers

Hiding in computer programs, there exists code that:

  1. estimates times to optimize schedules
  2. prioritises tasks while improving efficiency with mathematical analysis
  3. combines all activity times and finds available time slots using set theory and intersection calculations
  4. converts meeting times for different time zones by applying offsets

How Can We (Humans) Do That?

Although these tasks seem complex, you may already be completing these tasks everday:

  1. To estimate time, we often use online calendars. However, we can also write our activity times on paper.
  2. To prioritise tasks, we weigh their importance (list from least to greatest) while analyzing our productivity.
  3. Adding on to our first point that combines times, we can look for gaps of time in our day.
  4. Finally, we can convert to time zones by adding or subtracting hours / minutes from our times.
