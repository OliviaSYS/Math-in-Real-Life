Explore Math-Related Careers

Hover over the boxes and scroll inside of them to learn about a variety of careers involving mathematics!

Accountant

Task
Collecting, analysing, and reporting the financial data of the company they work for (e.g. calculate yearly profit, then examine trends)
Math Relation
This job requires complex mathematical calculations involving percents, ratios, fractions and decimals to ensure a successful business

Meterologist

Task
Observing and forecasting the weather
Relation to Math
Meteorologists make use of mathematical models of Earth’s physical features to predict the weather

Statistician

Task
Collecting and analyzing a company’s data / statistics
Relation to Math
Statisticians examine numerical data to help improve the company

Actuary

Task
Using financial theories and mathematical data to assess any future risks and help their clients develop policies to prevent failure
Relation to Math
This job makes use of calculus, statistics, problem-solving skills and probability in order to determine risks

Cost Estimators

Task
Collect and analyze price data to determine the time, money, materials, and work required for a product; further calculating the optimal selling price for it
Relation to Math
Cost estimators interpret mathematical data to keep the prices realistic

Software Engineers

Task
Programming software for companies to ensure any websites, apps, or systems work well; perhaps of their creation
Relation to Math
this occupation creates algorithms, appealing graphics and functional systems using logic, trigonometry, and linear algebra
